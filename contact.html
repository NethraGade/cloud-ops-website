Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the course?

Our CloudOps Master Program is an 8-week intensive course. Live classes are held on weekends (Sat/Sun) for 4 hours each, with additional lab sessions on Wednesday evenings. You should expect to dedicate ~10-15 hours per week including homework.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior professional experience is required, but basic computer literacy is essential. We start from the absolute basics of Linux and Cloud. If you know how to open a terminal, you are ready to start.

Is certification included?

We prepare you for the Microsoft AZ-400 (Designing and Implementing Microsoft DevOps Solutions) certification. While the exam fee is paid to Microsoft directly, our curriculum covers 100% of the exam topics.

What if I miss a live class?

All live sessions are recorded and sent to your student email address within 2 hours.
